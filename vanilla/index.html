# Score Win Bet Sum

Betting Strategy

The system determines the next bet based on the outcome of the previous hand.

Bet on the Streak (S)

If the absolute score delta between Banker and Player is 5 or greater (Δ ≥ 5):

We bet on the streak (same as previous hand winner/outcome) for the upcoming hand.

Bet on the Opposite (O)

If the absolute score delta between Banker and Player is less than 5 (Δ < 5):

We bet on the opposite of the previous hand winner for the upcoming hand.

Tie Outcomes

Ties are ignored or skipped entirely for betting decisions and are bypassed in the performance chart.
